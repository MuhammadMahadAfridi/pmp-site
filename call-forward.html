Call Forwarding

New phone number? Multiple phone lines? Set up call forwarding and save money on your bills today.

Call Forwarding service allows you to seamlessly forward any calls made toward your parked phone over to any US, Canada, or Skype number of your choosing. You even tget to retain your old caller ID. Forwarding can be used with multiple phone lines - you can choose to either receive all the calls on a single telephone, or through a separate telephone for each number.
